Lyric Rush
Welcome to Lyric Rush a game that tests your knowlegde of the 1920s!

There are two teams, when the game starts your team will be randomly assigned 1 of 6 songs and 12 of 30 questions.
Each time will have a chance to answer a question one at a time, upon answering a question correctly, a line in the song will be revealed.
When both teams have finished their questions, whichever team has more of their song revealed wins. The losing team has to sing the winning team's song.

The loosing team has an option to turn the tables... if they can guess the name and main writer of the song when given a possible list, the winning team must sing instead.
